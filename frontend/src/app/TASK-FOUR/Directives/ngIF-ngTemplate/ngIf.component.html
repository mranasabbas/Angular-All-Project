Structural directives

Structural directives change the structure of the DOM by adding or removing elements. They are recognizable by the asterisk (*) before the directive attribute.



A structural directive that conditionally includes a template based on the value of an expression contained to a Boolean value. When the expression evaluates to true, Angular renders the template provided in, and when false, Angular renders the template provided in an optional else.
